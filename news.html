HOD's Birthday Celebration

October 8, 2025

The Department of Architecture joyfully celebrated the birthday of Dr. Sule Adeniyi Olaniyan, our esteemed Head of Department. Faculty members and students came together to appreciate his visionary leadership, dedication to excellence, and inspiring mentorship. The event featured brief speeches, light refreshments, and photo sessions marking a memorable day.

Architecture Design Workshop

September 23, 2025

The Department hosted its annual Architecture Design Workshop, attracting students, professionals, and guest speakers from across the nation. Topics included sustainable urban planning, innovative design practices, and digital modeling techniques. The event encouraged collaboration between academia and industry leaders, fostering real-world learning experiences.

Best Student Award 2025

August 15, 2025

The Best Student Award recognizes outstanding academic achievement and creative excellence in architectural design. This year’s honorees were celebrated for their innovative projects and contributions to the faculty’s research initiatives. The event highlighted the department’s commitment to nurturing young talents for the future of sustainable architecture.
