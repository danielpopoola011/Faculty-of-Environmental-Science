History of Architecture

The Faculty of Environmental Sciences is among the newly established faculties in the University, established in the 2013/2014 academic session. Thus, the Department of Architecture is one of the five (5) departments that constitute the Faculty, and it also commenced that same session with a total number of 29 students. The pioneer Coordinator was Architect Z. I. Adedo, while Dr. Architect Olaniyan currently holds the position of Head of department.

Motto: “Designing for People, Culture & Environment.”
