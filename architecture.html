HISTORY OF THE DEPARTMENT

The Faculty of Environmental Sciences is among the newly established faculties in the University, established in the 2013/2014 academic session. Thus, the Department of Architecture is one of the five (5) departments that constitute the Faculty, and it also commenced that same session with a total number of 29 students. The pioneer Coordinator was Architect Z. I. Adedo, while Dr. Architect Olaniyan currently holds the position of Head of department.

Head of Department (HOD)

Dr. Sule Adeniyi Olaniyan

Discipline: Architecture

Position: Senior Lecturer, Department of Architecture, University of Ilorin

Research Focus: Sustainable architecture in the tropics, with emphasis on low-impact building materials, sustainable construction methods, and the use of Building Information Modelling (BIM) for building environment simulation and analysis.

Professional Membership: Registered Architect (ARCON F/2793), Full Member of the Nigerian Institute of Architects (M2769)

Academic Qualifications:

  • PhD in Sustainable Building Material (2017)
  • MSc in Sustainable Architecture (2012)
  • Bachelor’s in Architecture & certifications in Computer-Aided Design
